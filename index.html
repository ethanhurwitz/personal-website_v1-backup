Greetings! My name is Ethan Hurwitz and I am a Ph.D. student in University of California, San Diego's Psychology Department. By nature I am not big on having a social footprint, but have created this space to contain some basic information about my work and interests! Before my graduate studies I worked with Dr. Roland Griffiths at the Johns Hopkins Center for Psychedelic & Consciousness Research. Here at UCSD, I am a member of the UCSD Psychedelics and Health Research Initiative. My work seeks to characterize the cognitive mechanisms underlying psychedelic therapy. I am particularly interested in working to bridge the levels of analysis in psychedelic science.
